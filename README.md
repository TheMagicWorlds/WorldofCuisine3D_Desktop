## **📖Introduction**

Welcome to _Magic Worlds_ where everything is possible and the only limit is your imagination and dreams. Forever free, family friendly and open source.

_World of Cuisine_ is an immersive realm where food enthusiasts can explore diverse culinary cultures, experiment with recipes, and connect with virtual chefs. Whether you're crafting gourmet meals or discovering hidden food secrets, this world invites you to unleash your inner chef and embark on a gastronomic journey.

## **🚀How to Access World Cuisine**

1. **Launch Magic Worlds** and enter your spaceship.
2. **Talk to the Vice Captain** by walking up ahead and pressing `E`.
3. **Head straight up to the bridge** and enter by pressing `E`.
4. **Once in the bridge, climb up the platform** to access the travel console.
5. **Access the console** by entering the passcode `[789]`.
6. **Select World of Cuisine** in the World Hub.
7. **Start Exploring**: Dive into culinary workshops, food festivals, and interactive cooking challenges.

## **🎮Gameplay & Features**

- **🍜 Cooking Challenges** – Participate in time-based cooking contests to test your culinary skills.
- **🍲 Recipe Creation** – Experiment with ingredients to develop unique recipes.
- **👨‍🍳 Virtual Culinary Classes** – Learn techniques from AI-powered master chefs.
- **🍰 Food Festivals** – Join seasonal events and celebrate food cultures from around the world.
- **🎁 Unlockable Rewards** – Earn exclusive cooking tools, rare ingredients, and culinary collectibles.

### **🚨System Requirements**

#### Minimum:

- **OS**: Windows 10 (64-bit) / Linux / macOS 10.15+
- **Processor**: Intel Core i5-4460 / AMD FX-6300
- **Memory**: 8 GB RAM
- **Graphics**: NVIDIA GTX 760 / AMD Radeon R7 260X
- **DirectX**: Version 11
- **Storage**: 50 GB available space
